Heart Transplant Patient
Immunosuppressant Trough Protocol Active
📋
By 07:30
Verify trough order is present. If not, contact provider.
💉
08:30 – DRAW FIRST
Draw trough level before administering the morning dose.
Window: 08:15 – 08:45
💊
By 09:00 – THEN DOSE
Administer the scheduled dose immediately after the draw.
🚫
Do NOT draw trough with routine overnight labs (01:00 – 06:00)
⏲️
Do NOT hold the dose waiting for the lab result
⛔
Dose given BEFORE the draw = invalid trough. Contact transplant provider.
📝
MAR Documentation
Every dose: record the actual administration clock time (not the scheduled time).
If a dose is given late (>30 min off schedule): document the reason in the MAR note — procedure, NPO, pre-procedural, pt refused, transfer, medication delay, etc.
📞
Window missed, dose before draw, or timing unclear?
Contact transplant provider immediately
