Missed-Window Escalation

What to do when the trough timing window is missed

😬
Is this a heart transplant patient with a trough order today?
⏰

What happened?
📄

Routine Care

Not a trough day for this patient. No escalation needed.

✅

On Target

Trough was drawn in the correct window.

  • 💊 Administer dose by 09:00
  • 📝 Document actual last-dose time in MAR
  • ⏲ Do NOT hold dose for lab result
    • 🌙

    Escalate — Early-AM Draw

    A 01:00–06:00 AM draw is NOT a true trough. It's hours too early relative to the next dose.

  • 📞 Contact transplant provider immediately
  • 📋 Provider decides: accept level or order re-draw at correct time
  • 📝 Document the variance and provider decision in the chart
  • 💊 Do NOT hold the dose while waiting
    • ⛔

    Escalate — Dose Given Before Draw

    If the immunosuppressant was administered before the trough was drawn, the level is NOT a valid C0. It reflects post-dose pharmacokinetics, not steady-state trough — the number can mislead dosing decisions.

  • 📞 Contact the transplant provider immediately
  • 📋 Provider decides: discard level, or re-draw at the correct pre-dose window
  • 📝 Document the administration time, draw time, and the provider decision in the MAR
  • 💉 Document the reason the dose was given before the draw (procedure, home dose, admission, early admin)
    • ⚠️

    Escalate — Window Missed

    It's past 09:00 and no trough was drawn.

  • 📞 Contact transplant provider immediately
  • 📋 Provider decides: draw now and note delay, or skip today
  • 📝 Document the variance and provider decision
  • 💊 Administer the dose — do NOT continue to hold
    • 🔬

    Escalate — Clarify Timing

    There's ambiguity about dosing times or the medication schedule.

  • 📞 Contact transplant provider
  • 📋 Clarify actual last-dose time and next dose time
  • 📝 Document the clarification in the MAR
  • 🩸 Draw when the correct window is confirmed
